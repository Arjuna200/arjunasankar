How to make scrambled eggs


  1. Crack an egg and pour it in a bowl
  2. add salt to taste and beat the eggs
  3. heat the pan
  4. once the pan is heated slowly pour the egg mixture to the paan
  5. wait for 20 seconds
  6. using a spatula mix and tear the half formed ommlete
  7. serve the scrambled eggs in a bowl
