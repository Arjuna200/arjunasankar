My favorite places


Kerala,India

Kerala is located at india . for me kerala is a magical place filled with greenery and happiness
Kerala is known as a tropical paradise of waving palms and wide sandy beaches.
It is also known for its backwaters, mountains, coconuts, spices and art forms like Kathakali and Mohini Attam.
It is the most literate state in India, and a land of diverse religions


Georgia

Georgia is a country in eastern europe.
It is most known for it's wine and cheese.
Georgia is the oldest winemaking country in the world,
with some wine samples dating back to 6,000 years BC.
Georgia has a distinctive culture and a rich history that can be traced to classical antiquity and beyond.


Dubai,UAE

Dubai is one of the seven emirates that make up the UAE
It is essentially a desert city with superb infrastructure
Dubai makes a great short break for shopping, partying, sunbathing, fine dining and sporting events
The city is rather like an independent city-state and is the most modern and progressive emirate in the UAE
