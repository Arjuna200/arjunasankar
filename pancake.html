How to make pancake


  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together milk, butter (or oil), and egg
  3. add all-purpose flour,sugar,baking powder,salt annd whisk smoothly
  4. Heat a large skillet (nonstick or cast-iron) or griddle over medium
  5. For each pancake, spoon 2 to 3 tablespoons of batter onto skillet, using the back of the spoon to spread batter into a round
  6. Cook until surface of pancakes have some bubbles and a few have burst, 1 to 2 minutes.
    Flip carefully with a thin spatula, and cook until browned on the underside, 1 to 2 minutes more.
    Transfer to a baking sheet or platter; cover loosely with aluminum foil, and keep warm in oven.
    Continue with more oil and remaining batter. (You'll have 12 to 15 pancakes.) Serve warm, with desired toppings.
  7. enjoy your pancake
